Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Vital Bridge Healthcare Distribution Company ("Vital Bridge," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and corporate information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website or utilize our business services:

  • Professional Contact Information: Names, job titles, corporate email addresses, phone numbers, and physical business addresses.
  • Corporate Data: Company details, licensing information, and documentation necessary for SFDA regulatory compliance and market access applications.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and standard web analytics data collected through cookies to improve website functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly used for professional and operational purposes, including:

  • Facilitating business communications and strategic partnership inquiries.
  • Processing regulatory documentation for the Saudi Food and Drug Authority (SFDA).
  • Improving our website layout, user experience, and digital security.
  • Sending critical updates regarding supply chain logistics, contract statuses, or regulatory changes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Vital Bridge does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share necessary information only under the following circumstances:

  • With government bodies (e.g., SFDA, Ministry of Health) as strictly required for licensing, product registration, and customs clearance.
  • With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., secure logistics partners, IT infrastructure providers) bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our Terms of Use.

4. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Our digital infrastructure is designed to handle sensitive commercial and pharmaceutical data securely.

5. Your Rights

Under the PDPL, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by Vital Bridge. To exercise these rights, please contact our compliance team.
